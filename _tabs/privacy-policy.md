This blog is run by Ashish Ghimire at [ashishghimire.com](https://ashishghimire.com). This page explains what data is collected when you visit, and how it is used.

## What I Collect

I do not run any registration, accounts, or forms on this site. I do not collect your name, email, or any personal information directly.

The following third-party services are active on this site and may collect data independently:

### Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics (property ID: G-K3ZE89DCP9) to understand how visitors find and use the site — pages visited, time on page, referral source, and approximate location (country/city level). This data is aggregated and anonymous. Google's data practices are covered by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

You can opt out using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Google AdSense

This site displays ads served by Google AdSense (publisher ID: pub-3498505696958660). Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other sites. You can opt out of personalised advertising at [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).

### Umami Analytics

This site also uses a self-hosted instance of [Umami](https://umami.is/), a privacy-focused analytics tool. Umami does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information. It records page views and referrers in aggregate only.

### Giscus (Comments)

Post comments are powered by [Giscus](https://giscus.app/), which connects to GitHub Discussions. Leaving a comment requires a GitHub account. GitHub's privacy practices are covered by the [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

## Cookies

Google Analytics and Google AdSense set cookies in your browser. Umami and the core site do not. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

## External Links

Posts on this site link to external websites. I am not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.
